Overview

A luxury brand, built to convert.

Refined Build Construction needed a website that matched the quality and prestige of their work. The goal wasn't just to look premium — it was to guide visitors toward taking action: booking a consultation, requesting a quote, or reaching out directly.

Industry

Construction & Real Estate Development

Design to deployment, iterative build process

Deliverables

Responsive website · UI Design · Live Deployment via Vercel

The Challenge

Clean UX for lead generation.

"Construction companies often have cluttered, outdated websites. Refined Build needed to feel different — trustworthy, modern, and easy to act on."

The primary challenge was translating a high-end construction brand into a digital experience that felt premium without being cold or inaccessible.

Visitors needed to immediately understand what the company does, feel confident in their quality, and find it easy to get in touch — all within the first scroll.

Every design decision was made with one question in mind: does this make it easier for a potential client to say yes?

Process

How I approached it.

01

Discovery & Brand Direction

Started by understanding the client's target audience — high-end property developers and homeowners. Defined a visual direction: refined minimalism with strong typography and a natural, earthy palette that communicates trust and permanence.

02

Layout & Content Hierarchy

Mapped out the user journey from landing to conversion. Each section was structured to answer a specific question a potential client might have — who you are, what you build, how you work, and why to trust you.

03

UI Design & Component Build

Built using React + TypeScript with Vite for fast development. Tailwind CSS for utility-first styling with custom design tokens. Focused on consistent spacing, responsive breakpoints, and smooth interactions using Framer Motion.

04

Responsive Refinement & Deployment

Tested across mobile, tablet, and desktop. Fine-tuned typography scales, button sizing, and section padding for every breakpoint. Deployed to Vercel with custom domain configuration and performance optimization.
